About AppTrack

Welcome to AppTrack – your personal job application management tool!

Our Mission

Searching for a new job can be a challenging and time-consuming journey. With multiple applications to track, deadlines to remember, and statuses to update, it’s easy to lose sight of your progress. That’s where AppTrack comes in! AppTrack was designed to simplify your job search by providing an organized and user-friendly space to manage every aspect of your applications. Here, you can keep track of each position you apply to, monitor your application status, and store important details for every opportunity. With just a few clicks, you can update, edit, or delete entries, making it easy to keep your information current and focused.

Features

  • Add New Applications: Quickly add new job applications with details such as job title, company, date applied, and current status.
  • Track Progress: Update statuses as you progress through the application stages – from “Applied” to “Interviewing” to “Accepted” or “Rejected”.
  • Take Notes: Add custom notes for each job, such as key interview takeaways, follow-up actions, or additional insights.
  • Secure Access: Sign up for an account to securely access your application data, and rest assured your information remains private.

Why AppTrack?

AppTrack was built with job seekers in mind, whether you're applying for your first job or advancing in your career. We believe that having a streamlined, all-in-one platform can help take the stress out of managing multiple applications and help you stay organized throughout your job search. With AppTrack, your job application data is always at your fingertips, accessible from anywhere, and stored securely. We’re here to support you every step of the way as you move closer to your next career opportunity.
